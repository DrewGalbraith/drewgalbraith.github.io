Books

What I want to read next and what has been worth the time recently.

TBR

Dragonwatch (1 - 5)

Brandon Mull

We love Brandon Mull, and in an effort to keep our new hobby alive with a series we can agree on, my wife and I plan to read this together next.

Books I've Read

Fablehaven (1 - 5)

Dec 2024 - Feb 2025

Brandon Mull

Just as good as when I was a kid, and it was fun to read it with my wife. Pro tip: try doing voices for all the different characters.

Throne of Glass

Nov 2024

Sarah J Maas

This series came highly recommended. I read the first book and enjoyed it. I need to seek more books with female leads, I found the perspective refreshing. Overall, I'm moving away from violence though, so I opted not to coninue the series.

Wheel of Time (0 - 14)

Sept 2023 - Apr 2024

Robert Jordan (and Brandon Sanderson)

I wanted a challenge. And I was well-rewarded. I love the complexity that can come into a world when you give it 15 books to grow. I love that key characters died. That nations rose and fell. That no one person was 'the hero.' That said, I want a rewrite that doesn't talk so much about the dresses women wear and how all the women fold their arms beneath their breasts; my guy, that's where everyone folds their arms! The objectification is palpable.

Mistborn (1 - 3)

Apr 2023 - Aug 2023

Brandon Sanderson

This was a strong recommendation from friends. To be honest, I didn't love it. It was good enough. I like the first book best, and the rest were mildly interesting, but I just didn't care for the stiff romance nor the multiverse teases. The shape-shifting kandra, though? Master magic design. Good job, Brandon.

The Lion, The Witch, and the Wardrobe | The Magician's Nephew

May 2023

C.S. Lewis

I wanted to revisit a classic from my childhood and decided on this series. Overall, I found it too simple to really enjoy, and decided not to read the other 4 in the series, but it was entertaining regardless.

The Count of Monte Cristo

May 2023

Alexandre Dumas (and Auguste Maquet?)

The book was phenomenal. While I couldn't follow the French history, I loved the political intruigue. I can't imagine what the unabridged version possibly contains at 3x the abridged version.

Lord of the Rings 1 - 3

Mar 2023 - Apr 2023

J.R.R. Tolkein

I love these movies. I'd only read the Hobbit. My take on the book? If the scenery gets overwhelming or someone starts singing, skip ahead. Then the books are amazing. There's so much, particularly in the 3rd book, that I wish I'd been able to see in movies.

Stung

Aug 2022

Bethany Higgins

This was a darker dystopic America, not a vibe I usually dip into, but variety is the spice of life. It had my heart pounding as I canoed through Yellowstone back country.
